## Quality Observation: The Same-Day Duplicate

The handling of the same-day duplicate session is a useful protocol example. The right move was:

1. **Identify the duplicate before extracting.** Reading the first 20 lines of both transcripts revealed they were the same session.
2. **Confirm via existing source coverage.** Grep for the older folder name surfaced 11 articles already drawn from the session.
3. **Add the alternate title to the `sources:` of every article** (frontmatter and bottom-of-article both).
4. **Diff-check for missed material.** Read each existing article to confirm the session content was captured. Found one distinction worth creating new.
5. **Create the missing article.** `functional-vs-dysfunctional-relationship-configuration` captures the functional/dysfunctional contrast that the prior absorption had not distilled.

This is the protocol future agents should follow when encountering duplicate transcript folders.
