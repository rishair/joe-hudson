# Emotions Live in Muscles

## Definition

Feelings are a muscular event. Emotions are stored, expressed, and suppressed through muscle contraction. A person who was told they cannot be angry and now suppresses anger has muscles that contract in specific, observable ways. Over time, those contractions become chronic patterns visible in posture, facial expression, and movement.

"Our feelings live in our muscles. If you're the person who was told you can't get angry, your muscles have to contract in such a way and become distorted in such a way." -- Joe Hudson, Empathy

## In Practice

The way a person walks into a room reveals a tremendous amount about their emotional history and current state. Once trained to see it:
- How someone hunches their shoulders reveals what they are carrying.
- How someone tucks their butt reveals what they are holding.
- How someone purses their lips reveals what they are suppressing.
- How someone locks their jaw reveals what they are not saying.

This is the basis of body language and microexpressions. It is why mirror neurons work: the body broadcasts emotional state continuously.

When the body is rigid, empathy shuts down. Rigidity is the body's way of not feeling. Any form of emotional avoidance (distance, dissociation, wanting the emotion to stop) creates rigidity in the system. Common locations: belly, shoulders, jaw.

## Why This Matters for Coaching

Reading emotional state through the body is faster and more accurate than listening to words. A skilled coach taps someone's jaw to remind them it is locked. Hits are gentle, physical attention-redirectors, not corrections.

The reverse is also true: keeping the body loose forces feeling. You cannot avoid an emotion with a relaxed body. This is why [[staying-in-your-body]] is the primary empathy practice. A loose body is an empathic body.

## Upstream (What Leads Here)

- When someone describes feeling numb or "not emotional" but their body tells a different story.
- When a conversation is intellectually fluent but emotionally flat.
- When someone is stuck and the path is to move from head to body.
