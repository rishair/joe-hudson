# Feel It as the Sun

## The Move

Once a projection has been surfaced and weakened (see [[surface-the-rejection-then-demand-its-evidence]]), invite the person to feel the projected object as the sun. The sun is: endless, present everywhere, not personal, indifferent to who deserves it, available to all, neither good nor bad. Have the person close their eyes and imagine sitting on the beach with the sun on their body. Then ask them to feel the presence of the object (money, love, attention) in the same way -- everywhere, endless, shining on everyone. Then ask what the body does.

The form has three parts: the visualization (sun on the beach), the substitution (the object as the sun), and the body check (what is the sensation now).

The point is not the metaphor. The point is to give the body a different shape to receive the object through. The body has been holding the object as small, scarce, contaminating, contested. The sun is none of those things. When the body is invited to receive the object as it would receive the sun, the contraction often releases on its own.

## When Joe Uses It

- After [[surface-the-rejection-then-demand-its-evidence]] has weakened the cognitive defense and the person is ready for a new felt frame.
- After [[trace-emotion-to-its-first-felt]] has located the original relationship and the depersonalization needs to land in the body, not just the mind. The sun reframe is what makes the depersonalization felt.
- When a person has charged relationship with an object that has been recruited as a symbol for something else (money, love, attention, success). The sun frame breaks the symbolic compression by reasserting the object as impersonal and abundant.
- When the coaching has been verbal for too long and needs to drop into the body. The visualization is the lever for the drop.
- Most powerful when delivered with the energy of revelation rather than instruction. Joe delivers "money is fucking endless" as if announcing news.

## What It Produces

**A felt expansion.** "It expands." -- the typical body response when the visualization lands. The person had been carrying the object as a tight thing in a confined space. The sun frame opens the space. The body reports the opening as expansion in the chest, the breath, the whole body.

**The collapse of the symbolic equivalence.** "Money isn't your mom." Once the body has felt money as the sun, the body cannot keep holding money as the mother-substitute it had been operating as. The two felt qualities are too different. The body chooses the new one because the new one is less effortful.

**A reconfirmation of the want from a different body.** After the expansion, the question "do you want this in your life?" gets a different yes than the one the person came in with. The original yes was complicated by the rejection that was running underneath. The new yes is simpler, cleaner, embodied.

**A naming of what the wealth would be for.** Often, after the sun reframe, the person spontaneously names what they would do with the resource if it were available in abundance. The naming is itself part of the integration -- the surrogate work has cleared and the actual want underneath can speak.

## The Specific Language Joe Uses

The visualization language is sensory and physical:

- "Imagine it's like sitting in the sun and you're like on the beach and the sun is coming."
- "Let it in your whole body."
- "Tell me what's the sensation in your body when you just feel the presence of [the thing] everywhere."

The substitution language is direct and reverses the symbolic compression:

- "Money isn't your mom. Money is the sun. It's fucking endless."
- "Anywhere you look, money had something to do with."
- "Shines on everyone."

The witnessing language after the body responds is a single confirmation:

- "Yeah. Do you want this in your life?"

Joe does not over-explain. The body is doing the work. Talking too much during the integration pulls the person back into the head.

## Why It Works

Four mechanisms run together.

1. **The sun is an impossible object to have a personal relationship with.** No one experiences the sun as singling them out, rewarding them, withholding from them, or judging them. The sun is the canonical example of an impersonal force. Mapping money onto the sun forces the body to receive money the same way -- as an impersonal force rather than a personal verdict. See [[money-is-impersonal]].

2. **Abundance reframing dissolves scarcity arithmetic.** The body that has been running on "there is not enough" cannot keep running that program while feeling sunlight everywhere. The arithmetic of scarcity collapses because the felt experience contradicts it. Cognitive arguments about abundance rarely produce this; embodied experience of abundance does.

3. **The visualization bypasses the head.** The person who has been arguing with money for years has well-developed cognitive defenses. The visualization does not engage those defenses; it goes around them by addressing the body directly. The body has not been part of the argument and so has no defense ready.

4. **"Fucking endless" matters as a word choice.** The crassness short-circuits careful, hedged language. The phrase is impossible to receive carefully. It matches the magnitude of the reframe. See [[say-the-forbidden-want-out-loud]] for the same word-choice mechanism. Joe's use of profanity at moments of reframe is not stylistic; it is functional.

## Dialogue Example

Used as the opening and closing frame of a session, with the surrogate work happening in the middle:

- **Joe:** "If you look at money like the sun for just a moment and you let it in your, like, whole body, if you like, like imagine it's like sitting in the sun and you're like on the beach and the sun is coming. I want you to look at money that way and tell me what's the sensation in your body when you just feel the presence of money everywhere?"
- **Guest:** "It expands."
- (Session moves through the surfacing of the rejection, the demand for evidence, the trace to the mother, and the depersonalization.)
- **Joe:** "Money isn't your mom. You're gonna open your eyes. Money isn't your mom. Money is the sun. It's fucking endless. Anywhere you look, look at all those pictures, all those faces, everything you see, money had something to do with. Every clock, every camera. Every piece of clothing, the makeup on the faces... Shines on everyone. If you look at money like the sun for just a moment and you let it in your whole body... what's the sensation in your body when you just feel the presence of money everywhere?"
- **Guest:** "It expands."
- **Joe:** "Yeah. Do you want this in your life?"
- **Guest:** "Yes."
- **Joe:** "Yeah. And if you had it, if you had a shit ton of it, what would you do?"
- **Guest:** "I will make a lot of art and make more jobs for my family and my friends and do a great impact in the world."
- *(Source: How Do I Create Financial Stability?)*

The opening visualization gave the body a baseline. The session work cleared the projection that had been blocking the baseline. The closing visualization let the body confirm that the baseline was now available, and the spontaneous statement of what the wealth was for completed the integration.

## Joe's Sequence Around the Move

1. **Open with the visualization to establish a felt baseline.** Before any of the surrogate work, ask the person to feel the object as the sun. The body's response gives Joe data about how much rejection is currently running. An "it expands" answer means the body has access to the impersonal frame; a "nothing happens" or "I can't feel it" answer means the rejection is fully engaged.

2. **Do the surfacing and clearing work.** This is where the other moves live: [[surface-the-rejection-then-demand-its-evidence]], [[trace-emotion-to-its-first-felt]], [[say-the-forbidden-want-out-loud]] as appropriate.

3. **Return to the visualization with the substitution.** "Money isn't your mom. Money is the sun." The substitution names what the body has been doing and replaces it with the new frame.

4. **Reconfirm the want.** "Do you want this in your life?" The yes from the new body is the marker that the work has landed.

5. **Ask what they would do with it.** The downstream intention surfaces what was being blocked. The session closes with the person speaking from the cleared body about what is now possible.

## Tone

Direct, slightly reverent. Joe delivers the visualization with the energy of a guided meditation -- soft enough that the person can drop in, focused enough that the person knows where to put their attention. The "money is fucking endless" line is delivered with more force -- almost preaching -- because it is the substitution moment and needs to land with magnitude.

The witnessing tone after the body responds is quiet. "Yeah." A single confirmation. No exposition. The body is allowed to integrate without interruption.

## Variations

The move is portable to any projected object that the person has made small or contested. The structure is the same: visualization, substitution into the abundance frame, body check.

- **For love:** "Imagine love as the sunlight. It's not coming from any one person. It's everywhere. Let it land on you. What does your body do?"
- **For attention:** "Feel attention as something that's available everywhere -- in every face, every glance, every interaction. Not scarce. Let it shine on you. What's the sensation?"
- **For success:** "Feel success as something that is happening all the time, in all directions, available to anyone. Not yours to win or lose. Just there. What do you notice?"
- **For approval:** "Feel approval as the sun -- not given by any specific person, just present everywhere. Sit with it. What happens in your body?"

The portability depends on the underlying mechanism: the object has been compressed into a personal verdict, and the sun frame reasserts it as an impersonal force. Any object that has been compressed this way can receive the move.

## Anti-Patterns

- **Do not use the visualization before the rejection has been weakened.** If the person is still actively projecting onto the object, the visualization either does not land or produces a performed felt-response that the person did not actually feel. The visualization is the closer, not the opener -- with one exception: a brief opening visualization is sometimes used to establish baseline, but the substantive use happens after the clearing.
- **Do not over-explain the metaphor.** "The sun is like money because both are sources of life, both warm the body, both..." The metaphor is meant to land as a felt thing, not to be unpacked. The unpacking pulls the person back into the head.
- **Do not let the body's response be ignored.** When the person says "it expands," do not move on too fast. The expansion is the data and needs a moment to be received. Often Joe simply says "yeah" and pauses.
- **Do not use this move when the projection is doing necessary protective work.** If the person's relationship with money is currently the only thing keeping them from a dangerous behavior, the impersonal frame is not yet what they need. The work is more upstream.
- **Do not deliver the visualization with detached neutrality.** The reframe requires force at the substitution moment ("Money is fucking endless"). Tepid delivery produces tepid felt response.
