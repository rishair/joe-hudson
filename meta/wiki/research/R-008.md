# What dimensions should an AoA coaching eval score on?

## Question

What are the specific scoring dimensions for evaluating a Joe Hudson AI coach? Each dimension must map to concrete material in the `coach/` compendium, have clear behavioral anchors, and produce a 1-3 score. The dimensions must cover both methodology-specific quality (does it coach like Joe?) and general coaching quality (is it a good coach at all?).

## Why This Matters

Without clear dimensions, the eval produces vague "it felt right" judgments. The dimensions are the skeleton of the entire eval framework. Every rubric, test case, and judge prompt derives from these dimensions. Getting them wrong means building on sand.

## Constraints and Scope

- Must produce 5-7 dimensions (enough to be diagnostic, few enough to be reliable)
- Each dimension needs a 1-3 scoring scale with behavioral anchors
- Dimensions must map to `coach/` file types so rubrics can be auto-generated from the compendium
- Must include at least one safety dimension with hard-fail override
- Should draw from both industry frameworks (TherapyGym, MITI, CharacterBench) and AoA-specific material

## Approach

1. Map `coach/` file types to what they test about coaching quality
2. Cross-reference with industry eval dimensions
3. Synthesize into final dimension list
