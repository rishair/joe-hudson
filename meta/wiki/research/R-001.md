# What platforms and feeds host Art of Accomplishment content?

## Question

What platforms and feeds host Art of Accomplishment content? Specifically: the YouTube channel URL, the podcast RSS feed URL, and any other platforms where Joe Hudson publishes or appears as a guest.

## Why This Matters

We cannot build a catalog without knowing where to look. This research determines the full scope of [[G-002]]. If we miss a platform, we miss content. The answer also determines which download tools we need ([[R-003]]).

## Findings

### Primary Content Channels

**YouTube channel**: https://www.youtube.com/@ArtofAccomplishment. The channel hosts video versions of podcast episodes, standalone teachings, and the "Official AOA Masterclass" playlist (https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PLrbct081G13-lt3U3utRObf17j_oIrbdN). Subscriber count and total video count were not extractable from the page via fetch.

**Podcast (RSS)**: https://feeds.simplecast.com/zksImfUP. Hosted by Simplecast. The feed contains approximately 160 numbered episodes plus unnumbered "Bite-Sized Teaching" shorts. The most recent episode in the feed is Episode 160, "The Fear of Being Seen" (2026-05-08). The oldest episodes visible in the feed date to late December 2025, meaning the RSS feed exposes only the most recent ~25 episodes at a time. Older episodes exist but are not in the current feed window. The podcast launched in late 2020 (Apple Podcasts ID 1540650504).

**Podcast distribution**: The same feed powers listings on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, Audible, Overcast, Podbean, and standard podcast apps. These are not separate content sources; they all pull from the Simplecast RSS feed.

**Official website**: https://www.artofaccomplishment.com/podcast hosts episode pages with show notes. This may contain a more complete episode archive than the RSS feed window.

**Vimeo**: https://vimeo.com/user125574855. The account exists. Content scope is unknown; it may host course material or supplementary videos. Needs further investigation.

### Out of Scope (Paid/Private)

Art of Accomplishment offers paid courses and a membership community called "The Council." These include yearly Leadership Summits, Monthly Forums, group coaching sessions, and members-only events. This content is behind a paywall and out of scope per [[G-002]] constraints.

## Implications

1. **Two primary download targets**: The YouTube channel and the podcast RSS feed are the two main sources of freely available long-form content. All other podcast platforms (Spotify, Apple, etc.) are mirrors of the same RSS feed.

2. **RSS feed is windowed**: The Simplecast feed only exposes ~25 recent episodes. To catalog all ~160+ episodes, we will need to either (a) scrape the official website's podcast page for the full archive, or (b) use a podcast index API (e.g., Podcast Index, Listen Notes, Podnews) that has historical data. This is a key finding for [[R-003]].

3. **Guest appearances are scattered**: There is no single feed for Joe Hudson's guest appearances. Cataloging these requires manual search. Listen Notes (https://www.listennotes.com/top-podcasts/joe-hudson/) provides a partial index. A thorough catalog should include these, as they contain unique content not on the AoA channels.

4. **Vimeo is an unknown**: The Vimeo account exists but its content scope is unclear. An experiment should verify what is hosted there and whether it duplicates YouTube content or contains unique material.

5. **Download tooling needs**: YouTube content requires yt-dlp. Podcast episodes require either RSS download or website scraping. Guest appearances require per-show downloads (mostly available on YouTube or podcast apps). This informs [[R-003]].
