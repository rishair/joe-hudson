# Resistance Is the Thing You Fear, Not the Emotion

## Definition

The thing people are afraid will happen if they feel an emotion is actually what happens when they resist the emotion. The resistance produces the feared outcome, not the emotion itself. People have confused the resistance with the feeling.

## The three confusions

**Anger.** The fear: "If I allow my anger, I will destroy people." The reality: resisted anger destroys. Unresisted anger sounds like clear determination. "When there's no kinks in that garden hose, when it's fully allowed, it sounds like the anger of a Gandhi or Martin Luther King. It's clear, it's determined: we will not put up with this." -- Joe Hudson, Feel over Figure

**Sadness.** The fear: "If I allow my sadness, it will go on forever." The reality: resisted sadness lasts forever. That is what depression looks like. Allowed sadness moves through in minutes.

**Fear.** The fear: "If I allow my fear, I will be frozen and unable to act." The reality: resisted fear produces anxiety and freezing. Allowed fear shifts to excitement and mobilizes action.

"The reason that they feel that way is because that's how the resistance is. When you're resisting anger, you do destroy shit. And when you are resisting sadness, it does last forever. It looks like depression. And when you're resisting fear, then you're anxious all the time and you are frozen." -- Joe Hudson, Feel over Figure

## The garden hose

Emotions flow like water through a garden hose. Resistance is a kink in the hose. The kink produces distortion: passive aggression, rage explosions, chronic depression, anxiety. Remove the kink and the emotion flows clearly and resolves quickly.

"You're never overtaken by the feelings. You're overtaken by the resistance to the feelings." -- Joe Hudson, Feel over Figure

## When Someone Experiences This

When anger feels dangerous. When sadness feels bottomless. When fear feels paralyzing. The belief is that the emotion itself is the threat. The reality is that the resistance to the emotion is producing the exact experience being feared. See [[love-the-resistance]].
