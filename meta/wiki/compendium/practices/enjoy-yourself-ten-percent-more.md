# Enjoy Yourself 10% More

## Definition

A practice that works in any moment, regardless of circumstances. The instruction: allow yourself to enjoy whatever you are experiencing just a little bit more. Not a lot. Not "be happy." Just 10% more enjoyment than you are currently allowing.

## In Practice

The instruction is simple enough to feel absurd. But the body responds immediately.

"Right now I can say to everybody who's listening to this, hey, enjoy yourself just a little bit more right now. Just a little. Just allow a little more enjoyment in at this moment." -- Joe Hudson, Enjoy over Manage

"My entire body just relaxed a little bit." -- Brett Kistler's response.

Nothing about the external situation changes. The bank account is the same. The relationship is the same. The job is the same. And yet the body relaxes, presence increases, and the felt sense of the moment shifts.

## How to practice

1. At any moment, say to yourself: "Enjoy yourself just a little bit more right now"
2. Notice what happens in the body without trying to make anything happen
3. Do not try to enjoy a specific thing. Allow enjoyment to arise from wherever it wants to
4. Repeat throughout the day. No special conditions required

This is not about forcing a smile or faking positivity. If that is happening, it will feel less enjoyable, not more. The practice is an allowing, not a doing.

## Why it works

Enjoyment is a capacity in the nervous system that can be built, like a muscle. It is not dependent on conditions. It is an internal permission. Most people are unconsciously restricting their enjoyment because of a belief that they do not deserve it, or that enjoying themselves is irresponsible, or that there is something wrong that must be addressed first.

The 10% instruction bypasses these objections because it is too small to trigger resistance. You are not being asked to be happy. You are being asked to allow a fraction more of what is already available.

## When Someone Experiences This

When someone is in management mode and cannot find the off switch. When the body is tense and the mind is racing. When meditation feels like torture. When someone cannot access [[vulnerability]] or [[wonder]] because they are too constricted. This practice loosens the constriction enough for other work to begin.

## Upstream (What Leads Here)

- When [[enjoy-over-manage]] has been introduced and the person needs a concrete entry point

- [[wonder]] becomes more natural (less constriction)
