# Build Joe Hudson coaching compendium from all transcripts

## Outcome

An interconnected wiki of Joe Hudson's coaching methodology at `coach/`. Every concept, pattern, technique, and framework Joe uses is captured as its own article. Articles link to each other the way Joe's mind moves through a coaching session -- the structure itself encodes coaching navigation. A chatbot reading this wiki can understand what's happening with a user and navigate to the right remediations and coaching moves.

## Correctness

### Must-haves

- Every transcript (~480 total) has been read and its coaching content absorbed into the compendium
- Articles are organized into directories by type (initial hypotheses: `concepts/`, `patterns/`, `techniques/`, `frameworks/`, `examples/` -- actual taxonomy to be determined by R-004)
- Each article has YAML frontmatter with: type, id, title, related (list of linked article IDs), and tags
- Articles link to each other via `[[wikilinks]]` that encode coaching navigation -- links are directional and labeled (e.g., "when Joe encounters X, he moves toward Y"), not just "see also"
- Each article includes what connects to it upstream (what leads here) and downstream (where Joe goes from here)
- Concepts are grounded in Joe's actual language and direct quotes, not paraphrased into generic self-help
- Each article distinguishes what Joe teaches from what a guest or student says
- Encyclopedic tone (Wikipedia, not AI). Facts and direct quotes, no editorializing
- A master index at `coach/_index.md` with all articles organized by type, with aliases for query matching
- A backlinks file at `coach/_backlinks.json`
- Minimum 15 lines per article
- Each article cites transcript sources (episode name)

### Nice-to-haves

- Frequency data: which concepts appear most across transcripts
- A top-level "coaching flow" article that maps the general shape of how Joe moves through a session
- Distinction between Joe's evolved views (later episodes may refine earlier positions)
- Embeddings or vector representations suitable for RAG-based chatbot retrieval

## Exit Criteria

- [ ] All ~480 transcripts have been processed (tracked via `coach/_absorb_log.json`)
- [ ] Article taxonomy has been validated by research and refined at least once
- [ ] Every article has at least 2 directional navigation links to other articles
- [ ] `_index.md` and `_backlinks.json` are current
- [ ] At least 50 articles exist across all categories
- [ ] Prototype chatbot query test: given 5 sample user concerns, navigating the compendium produces relevant coaching guidance

## Context

G-001 produced ~480 speaker-diarized transcripts of Joe Hudson's podcast, courses, and coaching sessions. This goal transforms that raw material into structured coaching knowledge. The compendium is the knowledge base for a Joe Hudson coaching chatbot -- when a user describes a problem, the chatbot navigates the compendium to understand what's happening, what Joe would notice, what he'd do, and the larger framework the situation fits into.

The structure encodes navigation: articles don't just describe concepts in isolation, they link directionally. "When someone presents X, Joe typically notices Y" is a link from X to Y. The web of links IS the coaching intelligence.

Inspired by Farzaa's personal Wikipedia skill, adapted for coaching methodology extraction.
