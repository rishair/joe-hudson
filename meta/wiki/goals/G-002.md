# Discover and catalog all Joe Hudson / Art of Accomplishment content sources

## Outcome

A complete catalog file at `meta/wiki/catalog.md` listing every known Joe Hudson content item with:
- Title
- Publication date
- Platform (YouTube, Apple Podcasts, Spotify, guest appearance, etc.)
- URL or feed entry identifier
- Participants (if known)
- Duration (if known)

The catalog should distinguish between: (a) content on the Art of Accomplishment YouTube channel, (b) Art of Accomplishment podcast episodes, (c) guest appearances on other channels/podcasts.

## Context

We cannot download what we haven't found. This sub-goal must produce a comprehensive list before bulk downloading begins. Joe Hudson's content may span multiple platforms and formats. The YouTube channel and podcast are the primary known sources, but he may appear as a guest elsewhere.
