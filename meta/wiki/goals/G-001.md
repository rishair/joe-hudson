# Download and transcribe all Art of Accomplishment content with speaker diarization

## Outcome

Every publicly available Joe Hudson video and podcast episode exists locally as:
- The source media file (audio or video)
- A transcript with speaker diarization where Joe Hudson's voice is labeled distinctly from guests/co-hosts
- A `MEDIA.md` file with: title, publication date, participants, and general topic

Organized as `transcripts/{YYYY-MM-DD}_{title}/` with `MEDIA.md` and `transcript.md` per item.

Joe Hudson is the founder of Art of Accomplishment. His content appears on:
- A YouTube channel called "Art of Accomplishment"
- A podcast also called "Art of Accomplishment" (available on Apple Podcasts, Spotify, etc.)
- Possibly guest appearances on other podcasts/channels

The scope is all publicly available content. Not behind paywalls (unless freely accessible).

## Context

This is the root goal and is immovable. The model's knowledge cutoff is May 2025. Joe Hudson and Art of Accomplishment may have published new content since then, and platform access methods (yt-dlp, podcast RSS feeds, etc.) may have changed. Discovery and downloading will require active research and experimentation.

Agent context windows are limited. Multiple agents will work on this over time. The wiki is the only shared memory. Write everything down.
