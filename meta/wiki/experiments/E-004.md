# Check Vimeo account for unique content

## Hypothesis

The Art of Accomplishment Vimeo account (https://vimeo.com/user125574855) may host content not available on YouTube, such as course previews, older recordings, or supplementary teaching material. If it contains unique content, it becomes another source for the catalog.

## Method

1. Fetch https://vimeo.com/user125574855 using WebFetch. Extract: number of videos, video titles, dates, and any visible descriptions.
2. If the account has videos, compare titles against the YouTube channel listing from [[E-001]]. Identify any videos present on Vimeo but not YouTube.
3. Check if videos are public or require a password/membership. Password-protected videos are out of scope.
4. If unique public content exists, record: title, date, URL, duration for each.

## Success Criteria

- **Success**: The Vimeo account contains 1+ public videos not available on YouTube. These are added to the catalog scope.
- **Neutral**: The Vimeo account is empty, mirrors YouTube exactly, or all content is password-protected. No new catalog entries, but the question is resolved.
- **Failure**: The Vimeo page cannot be accessed or returns an error.
