# Check Vimeo account for unique content

## Hypothesis

The Art of Accomplishment Vimeo account may host content not available on YouTube, such as course previews, older recordings, or supplementary teaching material.

## Method

Fetched the Vimeo profile page and multiple pagination pages using WebFetch. Extracted video titles, approximate dates, and page count. Compared content types against the YouTube channel listing from [[E-001]].

## Result

The Vimeo account at https://vimeo.com/user125574855 contains **73 public videos** across 7 pages, with 83 followers. The oldest video ("Connection Course: VIEW User Manual") dates to approximately 2021. The most recent ("Leadership Workshop") is from approximately mid-2025.

**Content categories observed across sampled pages:**

| Category | Examples | Count (approx) |
|----------|----------|-----------------|
| Course material | "Decisions Course User Manual," "Connection Course: VIEW User Manual," "Great Decisions Course Trailer" | 10+ |
| Q&A sessions | "Great Decisions Q&A with Joe Hudson," "Community Deep Dive Q&A 2024," "How to Make Great Decisions Q&A" | 5+ |
| Rapid Fire Coaching | "Fear of Overworking," "Being Seen," "Rapid Fire Coaching Joe" | 5+ |
| Transformation stories | "Edmond - An AOA Story of Transformation," "Andrea - An AOA Story of Transformation" | 3+ |
| Teaching clips | "How to reframe trauma," "How to stop letting fear drive your decisions," "Decision Making is Emotional" | 10+ |
| Testimonials | "Lulie - I Could Be With Everything," "Lulie - He Said How Proud Of Me He Was" | 5+ |

All 73 videos appear publicly accessible. No password-protection indicators were found in the page source.

## Analysis

The Vimeo content is almost entirely distinct from the YouTube channel. The YouTube channel (329 videos from [[E-001]]) hosts podcast episodes, masterclass content, and promotional clips. The Vimeo account hosts course supplementary material: user manuals, Q&A recordings, rapid-fire coaching sessions, transformation stories, and testimonials.

There is minimal overlap. A few titles (e.g., "Rapid Fire Coaching") may appear on both platforms, but the bulk of the Vimeo content (course manuals, Q&As, testimonial stories) does not appear on YouTube.

**Boundary conditions**: Only 25 of 73 videos were sampled across pages 1, 2, and 7. The full set of 73 was not title-checked against the YouTube catalog. Some videos may be duplicates or clips extracted from longer YouTube content. A full de-duplication would require extracting all 73 titles and comparing them systematically.

**Scope decision**: These 73 videos are supplementary course content, not core podcast/teaching episodes. They are valuable for a complete archive but are lower priority than the 329 YouTube videos and 167 podcast episodes for the transcription goal ([[G-004]]). Whether to include them in [[G-003]]'s download scope depends on how comprehensive the project aims to be.

## Dead Ends

None. The Vimeo page was accessible and returned useful data. The main limitation is that Vimeo is JS-rendered, so WebFetch only captures partial listings (the server-rendered first page). Full extraction would require yt-dlp (which supports Vimeo) or the Vimeo API.

## Next Steps

- If included, yt-dlp supports Vimeo downloads and could extract the full list and audio. This would be a separate experiment.
- For now, their existence is logged as part of the [[G-002]] catalog.
