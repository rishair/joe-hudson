# Use Listen Notes or podcast indexes to catalog guest appearances

## Hypothesis

Podcast index services and web search will surface guest episodes on other podcasts not on the AoA feed. Combined with the partial list from [[R-001]], this should yield a near-complete catalog.

## Method

Four sources were queried:
1. **Listen Notes** (https://www.listennotes.com/top-podcasts/joe-hudson/): WebFetch. Returned 10 results, but included false positives (different people named Joe Hudson). Useful for recent appearances.
2. **Podchaser** (https://www.podchaser.com/creators/joe-hudson-107ta7cWZt): Returned 403. Not accessible via WebFetch.
3. **player.fm** (https://player.fm/podcasts/joe-hudson): Returned 403.
4. **Web search**: Multiple queries with show names, year filters, and cross-references against [[R-001]]'s initial list. Most productive source.

Results were de-duplicated against the AoA podcast feed (167 episodes from [[E-002]]). The Charisma on Command crossover with Dr. K (2025-12-05) and the Downshift/Jerry Colonna episode (2025-07-18) were confirmed as AoA feed episodes, not unique guest content.

## Analysis

14 guest appearances meets the success threshold of 10+. The list is likely incomplete; Joe Hudson's guest appearances have accelerated in 2026 (8 of 14 are from 2026 alone), and new ones may appear at any time. The 2020-2021 period has zero guest appearances, consistent with the podcast launching in late 2020 and Joe's public profile growing later.

**Listen Notes quality**: Mixed. It returns results for anyone named "Joe Hudson" across all podcasts (e.g., iRacing, earnings reports). Filtering requires manual inspection. It found 5-6 real hits among 10 results.

**Podchaser and player.fm**: Both blocked WebFetch with 403 errors. These services require browser-based access or API keys.

**Web search**: The most reliable method. Targeted queries with show names from [[R-001]] confirmed most appearances and found several new ones (Be Well, Soul Game, Insight Incorporated, Coaches Rising).

**Boundary conditions**: This catalog reflects public podcast appearances only. Joe Hudson likely appears on private/paid channels (The Council membership, summits) not indexed by public services. The list is a snapshot as of May 2026; new appearances are likely given the accelerating pace.

## Dead Ends

- **Podchaser**: 403 error. Would need browser automation or API access.
- **player.fm**: 403 error. Same limitation.
- **Listen Notes name collision**: "Joe Hudson" is a common name. The aggregator returns false positives from unrelated podcasts (racing, finance). Cannot be used without manual filtering.
