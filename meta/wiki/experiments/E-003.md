# Use Listen Notes or podcast indexes to catalog guest appearances

## Hypothesis

Podcast index services (Listen Notes, Podchaser, player.fm) aggregate cross-show appearances. Searching these for "Joe Hudson" will surface guest episodes on other podcasts that are not on the AoA feed. Combined with the partial list from [[R-001]], this should yield a near-complete catalog of guest appearances.

## Method

1. Fetch https://www.listennotes.com/top-podcasts/joe-hudson/ using WebFetch. Extract all listed episodes: show name, episode title, date, duration.
2. Fetch https://www.podchaser.com/creators/joe-hudson (or search for his profile) for additional cross-references.
3. Search the web for `"Joe Hudson" "Art of Accomplishment" guest interview` with year filters for 2021-2026 to catch appearances not indexed by podcast aggregators.
4. De-duplicate against the AoA podcast feed. Some "guest" episodes (e.g., the Charisma on Command crossover) were also published on the AoA feed. Mark these as crossovers, not unique guest content.
5. For each unique guest appearance, record: host show name, episode title, date, URL (preferably a direct audio/video URL or platform link), and duration if available.
6. Save results to `meta/raw/guest-appearances.tsv`.

## Success Criteria

- **Success**: 10+ unique guest appearances identified with show name, episode title, date, and at least one URL each.
- **Partial success**: 5-9 appearances found, suggesting more exist but are hard to discover.
- **Failure**: Fewer than 5 found, or index services do not reliably surface cross-show appearances for this person.
