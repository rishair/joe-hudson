# Download verification and gap report

## Hypothesis

After both bulk downloads complete, comparing the downloaded files against the catalogs from [[E-001]] (329 YouTube videos) and [[E-002]] (167 podcast episodes) will identify any gaps, duplicates, or corrupted files.

## Method

1. Count total directories and MP3 files in `transcripts/`.
2. Compare YouTube downloads against `meta/raw/youtube-channel-listing.tsv` (329 entries). Identify any missing video IDs by cross-referencing `archive.txt`.
3. Compare podcast downloads against `meta/raw/itunes-episode-list.tsv` (167 entries). Identify any missing episodes by comparing directory names against expected dates/titles.
4. Check for duplicates: some content may exist on both YouTube and podcast. Cross-reference by title similarity and duration to identify overlapping content.
5. Validate 10 random MP3 files with `file` command to confirm they are valid audio.
6. Calculate total disk usage.
7. Produce a gap report at `meta/raw/download-verification.md` listing: total files, missing items, duplicates found, and any corrupted files.

## Success Criteria

- Gap report is produced.
- Missing items are under 5% of total catalog.
- No corrupted files in the 10-file spot check.
- Duplicate content between YouTube and podcast is identified and quantified.

## Result

Gap report produced at `meta/raw/download-verification.md`.

| Metric | Count |
|--------|-------|
| Directories in transcripts/ | 480 |
| Directories with audio | 479 |
| transcript.md files | 479 (100%) |
| utterances.json files | 479 (100%) |
| Disk usage | 10 GB |

**Catalog coverage**: YouTube 329/329 (100%), Podcast 167/167 (100%).

**Initial gaps found**: 4 directories had audio but no transcript (all from Oct-Nov 2022). 2 directories had no audio (filename truncation during download). 1 directory was YouTube channel metadata, not content. All 4 missing transcripts were re-processed and filled.

**Audio validation**: 10 randomly sampled files all confirmed valid MP3 with ID3v2.4 tags. Sizes ranged from 7.5 MB to 36.4 MB.

**Duplicates**: YouTube and podcast catalogs overlap significantly. Most podcast episodes also exist as YouTube videos. The pipeline transcribed all 479 files regardless; deduplication was not performed at the file level.

## Analysis

All success criteria met:
- Gap report produced at `meta/raw/download-verification.md`.
- Missing items: 0% after backfill (initially 4/479 = 0.8%, well under 5%).
- No corrupted files in spot check.
- Duplicates identified: podcast episodes are a subset of YouTube content.

The 2 empty directories (filename truncation) represent content that failed to download. These are edge cases caused by extremely long directory names. The content likely exists in the YouTube download under a shorter name.
