# Download verification and gap report

## Hypothesis

After both bulk downloads complete, comparing the downloaded files against the catalogs from [[E-001]] (329 YouTube videos) and [[E-002]] (167 podcast episodes) will identify any gaps, duplicates, or corrupted files.

## Method

1. Count total directories and MP3 files in `transcripts/`.
2. Compare YouTube downloads against `meta/raw/youtube-channel-listing.tsv` (329 entries). Identify any missing video IDs by cross-referencing `archive.txt`.
3. Compare podcast downloads against `meta/raw/itunes-episode-list.tsv` (167 entries). Identify any missing episodes by comparing directory names against expected dates/titles.
4. Check for duplicates: some content may exist on both YouTube and podcast. Cross-reference by title similarity and duration to identify overlapping content.
5. Validate 10 random MP3 files with `file` command to confirm they are valid audio.
6. Calculate total disk usage.
7. Produce a gap report at `meta/raw/download-verification.md` listing: total files, missing items, duplicates found, and any corrupted files.

## Success Criteria

- Gap report is produced.
- Missing items are under 5% of total catalog.
- No corrupted files in the 10-file spot check.
- Duplicate content between YouTube and podcast is identified and quantified.
